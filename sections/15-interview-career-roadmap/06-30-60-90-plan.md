# 30/60/90 Day Plan

## Why this lesson

Show interviewers you can ramp up and deliver impact after joining.

## Outcomes

- ✅ Concrete goals for first 30/60/90 days
- ✅ Learning and delivery milestones
- ✅ Metrics for success

**Time**: ~20 minutes

**Tasks
1) Draft goals for each time window
2) Include learning, building, and partnering objectives
3) Add metrics to measure success

## Deliverable

- 30/60/90 plan document

## Checkpoint

Can you articulate what you’ll deliver and learn in each phase?
