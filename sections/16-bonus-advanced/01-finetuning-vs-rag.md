# Fine-tuning vs RAG

## Why this lesson

Decide when to fine-tune vs use retrieval so you spend effort and budget wisely.

## Outcomes

- ✅ Criteria for choosing fine-tuning vs RAG
- ✅ Cost/performance considerations
- ✅ Decision checklist

**Time**: ~20 minutes

**Tasks
1) List scenarios favoring RAG vs fine-tuning
2) Estimate cost/time differences
3) Create a quick decision flowchart

## Deliverable

- Decision guide

## Checkpoint

Can you justify RAG or fine-tuning for a given use case in 60 seconds?
