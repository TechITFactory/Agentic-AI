# MLOps vs DevOps

## Why this lesson

Understand what changes when the workload is ML so you build the right controls.

## Outcomes

- ✅ Explain key differences (data, models, eval) vs DevOps
- ✅ Identify lifecycle stages unique to ML
- ✅ Map roles and responsibilities

**Time**: ~20 minutes

**Prerequisites**: Sections 5–6 deployments

## Agenda

- DevOps recap; where ML diverges
- Data/model lifecycle
- Ownership and interfaces

## Hands-on

- List three ML-specific risks and how you’d mitigate them

## Deliverable

- One-page comparison table for your team

## Checkpoint

Can you explain to a DevOps peer why model evaluation is part of CI?
