# Reranking (When Retrieval Isn’t Enough)

## Why this lesson
Improve relevance by reranking retrieved candidates before generation.

## Outcomes
- ✅ Explain reranking purpose
- ✅ Integrate a reranker into the pipeline
- ✅ Observe quality vs latency trade-offs

**Time**: ~20 minutes

**Prerequisites**: Retrieval improvement lesson

## Agenda
- What rerankers do
- Popular reranker options
- Latency considerations

## Hands-on
- Apply a reranker to top-20 and keep top-5
- Compare answer quality with/without rerank

## Deliverable
- Short note on quality and latency change

## Checkpoint
Is reranking worth the added latency for your use case?
