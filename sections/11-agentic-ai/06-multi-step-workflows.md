# Multi-Step Workflows (Tickets, PRs, Runbooks)

## Why this lesson
Coordinate several tool calls to complete real-world tasks.

## Outcomes
- ✅ Break tasks into steps with tool choices
- ✅ Handle failures and retries per step
- ✅ Summarize outcomes clearly

**Time**: ~20 minutes

**Prerequisites**: Memory lesson

## Agenda
- Planning strategies
- Step orchestration and branching
- Summaries for users

## Hands-on
- Draft a workflow for “analyze logs → suggest fix → open PR template”

## Deliverable
- Workflow plan with steps, tools, and exit conditions

## Checkpoint
Could someone implement your plan without guessing missing steps?
