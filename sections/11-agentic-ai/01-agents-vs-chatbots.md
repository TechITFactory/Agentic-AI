# Agents vs Chatbots

## Why this lesson
Differentiate simple chat flows from tool-using agents so you design the right architecture.

## Outcomes
- ✅ Define what makes an agent (tools, loop, memory)
- ✅ Identify when a chatbot is sufficient vs an agent is needed
- ✅ List risks unique to agents

**Time**: ~15 minutes

**Prerequisites**: Section 10 RAG and tool-calling basics

## Agenda
- Chatbot characteristics vs agents
- Agent capabilities and risks
- Use case decision guide

## Hands-on
- Pick two use cases; decide chatbot vs agent and justify

## Deliverable
- Short decision table for your project ideas

## Checkpoint
Can you explain why tool access changes the safety model?
