# Chunking Strategies

## Why this lesson
Chunking quality drives retrieval quality; pick strategies intentionally.

## Outcomes
- ✅ Compare fixed-size vs semantic chunking
- ✅ Understand overlap effects
- ✅ Choose chunking per document type

**Time**: ~20 minutes

**Prerequisites**: Similarity search lesson

## Agenda
- Fixed window vs recursive/semantic chunkers
- Overlap pros/cons
- Chunk metadata basics

## Hands-on
- Chunk a sample doc with two strategies; count chunks and average length
- Note which retains headings/context better

## Deliverable
- Brief note comparing chunk strategies and chosen defaults

## Checkpoint
Can you justify overlap size for a FAQ vs long-form doc?
